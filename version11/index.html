MDL Private Equity LLC is a limited liability corporation organized to assist existing small businesses located in Columbia, South Carolina and surroundings areas. We seek to enhance small businesses with growth and scalable potential by providing capital, organizational structure, and mentoring as needed. The ideal business will have at least $100,000 in sales (with some exceptions), an established business model, and potential for scalable growth. MDL Private Equity LLC is open to investing in any industry, product, or service. An investment from our corporation provides you with a strong business partner and positions your business for a better future. In return for capital invested in your business, MDL Private Equity LLC will ask for a small equity state.
