Desmond Lewis

Desmond Lewis is the co-founder of MDL Private Equity, LLC, where he is responsible for the day-to-day operations. He was born in Columbia, South Carolina and is a graduate of Columbia High School. In 1986, Mr. Lewis decided to serve his country by joining the United States Navy, where he served 12 years. After earning an honorable discharge from the United State Navy, Mr. Lewis furthered his education at Midlands Technical College where he studied business management. While pursuing his education, Mr. Lewis started a career in selling and investing in real estate. He has over 20 years of experience in marketing, negotiating, investing, and selling real estate. Mr. Lewis has been successful with business endeavors and his value lies in his strengths, which are creativity, attention to detail, and organizational skills.

Lisa Lewis
Lisa Lewis is the co-founder of MDL Private Equity, LLC, where she is responsible for evaluating investment opportunities and assist with implementing the company’s strategic goals. She was born in Eastover, South Carolina and is a graduate of Lower Richland High School. Mrs. Lewis obtained a Bachelor’s of Science in Business Administration, with a major in accounting, from the University of South Carolina. She has over 20 years of experience in the banking industry, where she has been responsible for evaluating the financial stability of financial institutions and assessing regulatory compliance with various laws and regulations. Mrs. Lewis has overcome life challenges that have made her the strong resilient person she is today. Her value lies in her ability to connect with people to help them find solutions to their challenges.
